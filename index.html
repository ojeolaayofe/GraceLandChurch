Services

GraceLand Church Services

These are what we stand for

Children and Youth Ministry

Our desire is that children and youth will experience the excitement and power of God whenever they come to church by creating an atmosphere that is nurturing and loving. Here at Graceland, children will not only have fun playing with their friends, but they will also have fun learning how to apply age appropriate scriptures lesson to their daily lives. In the midst of the chaos of adolescence, we walk faithfully with our youth as they transition from children to young adult by discipling them into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ for they are tomorrow leaders

Prison, Hospital and Homeless Ministry

Graceland is passionate about Jesus’ command, “Go into all the world and preach the gospel to all creation.” (Mk 16:15). We believe in the tangible expression of God love which is rooted in compassion for those who are forgotten and hurting, love in action opens doors for the gospel. It is a lifestyle of the church to attend to the people to whom we are called to serve: the homeless, in prison, and in hospitals.

Men and Women Ministry

Men and Women Ministry offers opportunities where we can gather together for prayer and fellowship in various settings. We seek to develop leaders according to Proverbs 27:17 “Iron sharpens iron, by imparting information and experiences that help them to grow into responsible mature christians. The ministry is dedicated to providing a place and atmosphere where we can share our lives, confident that others are there to support us. The ministry meetings: prayer breakfast, evening of bible study and times of just plain fun are for accountability and encouragement in faith and life.

Marriage and Family Ministry

Marriage & Family Ministry provides Godly guidance, counseling and mentoring to build healthy marriage and family. If you are planning to get married, Graceland offers pre-marital program that gives each couple effective Godly tools enabling your marriage to be the way God designed or intended it to be. The Parenting Ministry serves to provide inspiration, resources, and support to parents in their parenting journey. This class will open your eyes to biblical parenting principles and a clear game plan to raise your children to maturity.

Social Wellfare and Refuge Ministry

Graceland welcoming team encourages its members to respond to the needs of the refugees, asylum seekers and forcibly displaced persons to support the efforts of partner agencies to resettle refugees in US. We are dedicated to put in place activities and programs that help ease the journey of families eager to start their new life away from violence and fear. The overall aims is to help rebuild the life of these group of vulnerable persons. Activities and program include but are not limited to the provision of pro-bono legal aid services, psycho-social assistance, education program for children, as well as poverty alleviation and job initiatives.
